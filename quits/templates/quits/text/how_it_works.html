How does it work?

local_dining
You go out for a meal with your two housemates and one of them pays the bill. She makes a quick note of the amount in your AreWeQuits? group.

shopping_cart
After the meal you're in the red, so you offer to pay for the next household shop and add it to the AreWeQuits? group.

local_bar
Your other housemate is still in the red, so next time the three of you go out, he's buying the drinks!

done
And that's how it works! Everyone's debts get cleared without any money changing hands. If you need to settle a debt urgently, you can just add another payment from one person to others.
